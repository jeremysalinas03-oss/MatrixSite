The Architect’s Blueprint: My Tech Career Fields

Cyber Security

I want to defend critical systems, safeguard information, and stay one step ahead of threats that most people never even see. Working with agencies like the NSA or CIA would open the path to mastering this field and understanding just how deep the digital 'rabbit hole' goes.

Data Center Architecture

I’m drawn to this field because it represents the physical side of modern computing—the part most people never see. Understanding how power, networking, redundancy, and security come together to keep systems online is both challenging and essential. Companies like Amazon, Google, or Microsoft are constantly building and optimizing these environments, and I want to be part of that work.

Network Installer

What interests me most about this field is how precision and planning directly shape performance. Every cable, rack, and termination affects the reliability of the entire system. Whether it’s designing structured cabling, configuring switches, or ensuring clean fiber runs, the work demands both technical skill and attention to detail.
